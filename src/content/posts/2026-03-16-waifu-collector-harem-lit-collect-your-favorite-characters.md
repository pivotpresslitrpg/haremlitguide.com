## How the Waifu Collector Works

The core loop is simple and addictive. Every day, you get a **free daily pull** — one random waifu card added to your collection. Every week, you get a **weekly pack** of three cards. And when you first sign up, you get a **five-card welcome pack** to start your collection immediately.

But daily pulls are just the beginning. The Waifu Collector rewards you for actually using the site:

- **Write a review** on Harem-Lit.com? Bonus pull.
- **Level up** your account through site activity? Bonus pull.
- **Hit a login streak** of 7 days? Two bonus pulls. 14 days? Three. A full month? Five bonus pulls.
- **Redeem an author code** from your favorite writer? Up to five pulls per code.

Everything is earned. The Waifu Collector has zero pay-to-play mechanics — the team at [Harem-Lit.com](https://harem-lit.com) has been clear about this from the start. Every card has zero real-world monetary value. This is a collector's game built for fans, full stop.

## Six Rarity Tiers — From Common to Legendary to the Elusive Unique

Every waifu card in the collector falls into one of six rarity tiers, and the hunt for higher rarities is where the real collector's itch kicks in:

1. **Common** (gray frame) — Your bread and butter. You'll see these often.
2. **Uncommon** (green frame) — A step up. Nice to have.
3. **Rare** (blue frame) — Now we're talking. Guaranteed within 60 pulls via the pity system.
4. **Epic** (purple frame) — Hard to find, satisfying to own. Pity kicks in at 80 pulls.
5. **Legendary** (gold frame, holographic shimmer) — The cards that make you audibly react. Full holographic glow effects, custom animations, and a pity guarantee at 100 pulls. These are the crown jewels of any waifu collection.
6. **Unique** (diamond frame) — The rarest tier in the entire Waifu Collector. These can *only* be obtained through special author codes. No amount of daily pulling will get you one. You need to follow your favorite authors, watch for their giveaways, and be ready when a code drops.

The **pity system** deserves its own mention — it's genuinely player-friendly. If you haven't pulled a Rare card in 60 pulls, the system guarantees one. Same for Epic at 80 and Legendary at 100. No one gets left behind by bad luck forever.

And if you're the kind of collector who does **multi-pulls** (10 cards at once using accumulated bonus pulls), the last slot is guaranteed to be at least Rare. The Waifu Collector actively wants you to have a good time.

## The Shard System: What Happens to Duplicates

Every collector's nightmare is pulling a duplicate — but the Waifu Collector turns duplicates into a resource. When you pull a card you already own, it automatically converts into a **Shard** of that card's rarity tier.

Collect **10 Shards** of any rarity and you can redeem them for a card of your choice at that rarity — specifically, a card you're *missing* from your collection. This means duplicate pulls aren't wasted; they're progress toward completing your collection.

You can also **upgrade Shards** across tiers (trade up Common Shards toward Rare ones), manually **shatter** cards you don't want into Shards, or **bulk-salvage** all your duplicates at once. The system is designed so that every single pull moves you forward, even when luck isn't on your side.

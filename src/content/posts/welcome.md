HaremLit Guide is now live.

We're an independent editorial site covering the best in harem fantasy and men's romance fiction. The genre publishes hundreds of books a year — our goal is to help you find the ones worth your time.

## What We Cover

- **Ranked lists** — curated by community ratings and editorial judgment
- **Author spotlights** — deep dives into the authors worth following
- **New releases** — regular roundups of what just dropped
- **Genre guides** — whether you want isekai harems, dungeon core romance, fae fantasy, or modern supernatural

## Our Data

Book data comes from [Harem-Lit.com](https://harem-lit.com), the largest community database for the genre. Every list reflects genuine editorial curation built on real reader ratings — not just an algorithm sort.
